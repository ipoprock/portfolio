About Me

I’m Shubham Kushwaha, a 3D Game & Technical Artist dedicated to creating immersive, high-quality digital experiences that seamlessly combine art and technology.

With expertise in Unity, Unreal Engine, Blender, Maya, 3ds Max, SpeedTree, Substance Painter, Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, Premiere Pro, and many more professional tools, I specialize in asset creation, look development, texturing, shading, lighting, and optimization for real-time and cinematic production.

Alongside game and cinematic work, I also create realistic product visualizations for AR, VR, WebGL, and marketing applications, delivering high-poly and optimized low-poly models with clean topology, detailed textures, and perfect lighting for presentation and advertising.

I’ve contributed to high-profile CG series, feature films, and real-time projects, collaborating across departments to achieve seamless pipelines, optimized assets, and exceptional visual results for PC, console, AR, VR, and WebGL platforms.

Currently, I’m part of Maayaverse Creations, focusing on real-time visuals, shader development, and technical art tools that streamline workflows and enhance creative storytelling.

Driven by curiosity and innovation, I continuously explore new workflows, tools, and technologies to push the boundaries of digital art, ensuring every project I work on meets both artistic and technical excellence.
